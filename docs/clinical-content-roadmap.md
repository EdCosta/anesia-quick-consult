# Clinical Content Roadmap

## Universal Safety Checklist

- Content type: `protocoles`
- Canonical id: `universal-safety-checklist`
- Category: `safety`
- Tags: `who`, `safety`, `sign-in`, `time-out`, `sign-out`, `handoff`
- Structure:
  - Pre-op / sign-in checks
  - Intra-op / time-out checks
  - Post-op / sign-out and handoff checks
  - Local adaptation notes per site

## Perioperative Anticoagulation

- Content type: `guidelines`
- Canonical ids:
  - `anticoag-doac-decision`
  - `anticoag-antiplatelet-decision`
  - `anticoag-neuraxial-safety`
- Category: `safety`
- Tags: `anticoag`, `doac`, `anti-xa`, `antiplatelet`, `neuraxial`, `bleeding`
- Structure:
  - What drug is the patient taking?
  - Stop interval by renal function / bleeding risk
  - Neuraxial constraints
  - Restart timing
  - Red flags / call senior

## PONV

- Content types: `guidelines` + `protocoles`
- Canonical ids:
  - `ponv-risk-stratification`
  - `ponv-risk-prophylaxis`
- Category: `ponv`
- Tags: `ponv`, `apfel`, `ambulatory`, `antiemetic`, `rescue`
- Structure:
  - Risk score interpretation
  - Prophylaxis ladder by risk tier
  - Rescue if prophylaxis fails
  - Discharge triggers / escalation

## Difficult Airway

- Content types: `guidelines` + `protocoles`
- Canonical ids:
  - `difficult-airway-algorithm`
  - `difficult-airway-rescue-drugs`
- Category: `airway` for guidelines, `safety` for protocol
- Tags: `airway`, `difficult_airway`, `cico`, `rescue`, `videolaryngoscope`
- Structure:
  - Preparation and preoxygenation
  - Plan A / B / C / front-of-neck access
  - Team escalation and stop points
  - Rescue drug quick-reference aligned with local formulary

## ALR Block Enrichment

- Content type: `alr_blocks`
- Canonical field additions per block:
  - `sonoanatomy`
  - `usual_volumes`
  - `complications`
  - `red_flags`
- Tags: `alr`, `ultrasound`, `local_anesthetic`, region-specific tags
- Structure:
  - What to identify
  - Typical volume and concentration
  - Common complications
  - Immediate stop / urgent reassessment criteria
